Welcome to Abras Nigeria Enterprises

Nearly 30 Years of Excellence

Abras Nigeria Enterprises stands as a pillar of reliability in Nigeria's heavy-duty equipment industry. Registered on November 1st, 1996, we have built nearly three decades of expertise in supplying high-quality spare parts, rims, and tires for heavy-duty equipment and machinery. Our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure that Nigeria's industrial backbone remains strong through superior equipment support.

From our headquarters in Abeokuta, Ogun State, we have grown from a local supplier to a trusted partner for some of Nigeria's most prominent industrial companies. Our success is built on unwavering commitment to quality, rapid response times, and deep understanding of our clients' operational needs. We exclusively represent top-tier manufacturers, providing fit-for-purpose machines and genuine spare parts designed to meet our customers' specific application needs across all industries we serve.
