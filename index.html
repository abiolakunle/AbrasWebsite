Welcome to Abras Nigeria Enterprises

Nearly 30 Years of Excellence

Since 1996, we have been Nigeria's trusted supplier of premium spare parts, tires, rims, and comprehensive maintenance services for construction, mining, and industrial equipment. Our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure that Nigeria's industrial backbone remains strong through superior equipment support.

From our headquarters in Abeokuta, Ogun State, we have grown from a local supplier to a trusted partner for some of Nigeria's most prominent industrial companies. Our success is built on unwavering commitment to quality, rapid response times, and deep understanding of our clients' operational needs.

Authorized Supplier of Premium Brands

KOMATSU

Authorized supplier of genuine Komatsu parts with comprehensive inventory management. Our Komatsu expertise ensures optimal performance and longevity for your equipment, backed by the quality and durability synonymous with the Komatsu brand.

CATERPILLAR (CAT)

Complete range of genuine CAT parts from engines to undercarriage components. As a trusted Caterpillar supplier, we provide reliable solutions that ensure continued performance and productivity of your CAT machinery across all applications.

VOLVO

Genuine Volvo parts and components backed by manufacturer quality standards. Our Volvo inventory supports maintenance and repair needs across diverse industries, upholding the performance and longevity synonymous with Volvo engineering.

PERKINS

Comprehensive selection of genuine Perkins engine parts renowned for durability and efficiency. Our Perkins expertise ensures your equipment maintains optimal power output and fuel efficiency throughout its operational life.

CUMMINS

Genuine Cummins engine components trusted for power and reliability. Our Cummins inventory includes complete engine systems, fuel systems, electrical components, and cooling systems for maximum operational efficiency.
