Welcome to Abras Nigeria Enterprises

Nearly 30 Years of Excellence

Since 1996, we have been Nigeria's trusted supplier of premium spare parts, tires, rims, and comprehensive maintenance services for construction, mining, and industrial equipment. Our journey began with a simple mission: to ensure that Nigeria's industrial backbone remains strong through superior equipment support.

From our headquarters in Abeokuta, Ogun State, we have grown from a local supplier to a trusted partner for some of Nigeria's most prominent industrial companies. Our success is built on unwavering commitment to quality, rapid response times, and deep understanding of our clients' operational needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Services

What types of equipment do you supply parts for?
We supply parts for bulldozers, excavators, dump trucks, wheel loaders, motor graders, forestry machines, and utility equipment from leading manufacturers like Komatsu, Caterpillar, Volvo, Perkins, and Cummins.
Do you provide emergency breakdown support?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency breakdown support with rapid response services. Our mobile service units provide on-site repairs and emergency part replacement to minimize downtime.
How long does it take to get parts delivered?
We offer same-day processing for urgent requirements through our streamlined 4-step process. Delivery times depend on part availability and location, with real-time tracking provided for all orders.
Are all your parts genuine?
Yes, we exclusively supply genuine parts from top-tier manufacturers. All components are backed by manufacturer warranties and meet original equipment specifications for optimal performance.
What maintenance services do you offer?
We provide component overhaul, emergency breakdown support, technical troubleshooting, scheduled maintenance inspections, and fluid analysis services to maximize equipment uptime.
How long have you been in business?
Abras Nigeria Enterprises was established on November 1st, 1996. We have nearly 30 years of experience in the heavy-duty equipment industry, serving Nigeria's industrial backbone.
Which major companies do you work with?
We work with Nigeria's leading companies including Dangote Refinery, AG Dangote Construction, Arab Contractors, BUA Cement Sokoto, JB Farms, and Segilola Resources across various industries.
What industries do you serve?
We serve multiple industries including Construction, Mining, Agriculture, Infrastructure development, Oil & Gas, and Industrial Operations across Nigeria and West Africa.
Where are you located?
Our headquarters are located at 41, Ifeloju Street, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. We operate Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM, with emergency services available 24/7.
How can I get a quote for parts or services?
You can contact us at +234 803 677 5192 or email Abrasnigeriaent@gmail.com for quotes. We offer rapid order processing with same-day response for urgent requirements.
