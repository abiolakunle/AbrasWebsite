Our Team Profiles

Mr. Yemi Abiola - Founder & Managing Director

Visionary leader with extensive experience in heavy equipment industry. Founded Abras Nigeria Enterprises in 1996 with a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a local supplier to a trusted partner for Nigeria's most prominent industrial companies.

Mr. Omotayo Ibrahim - Procurement & Logistics Manager

Expert procurement professional managing supplier relationships and logistics operations. Ensures rapid sourcing and efficient delivery of parts and services. His expertise in supply chain management has been instrumental in establishing Abras as a reliable partner for urgent equipment needs.

Engineer Joseph Ilesami - Maintenance Department Head

Graduate of Industrial Maintenance Engineering with specialized expertise in heavy equipment systems. Oversees all maintenance and repair services with technical excellence. His engineering background ensures that all maintenance programs are executed to the highest technical standards.

Mr. Olakunle Abiola - IT Department Manager

Technology leader ensuring efficient systems integration and digital solutions for streamlined operations and customer service excellence. His innovative approach to technology has modernized Abras' operations, enabling real-time tracking and efficient order processing.
